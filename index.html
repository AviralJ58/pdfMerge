Multi File Support

Unlike other apps, pdfMerge is designed to merge different file types (DOC,PPT,PDF) in a single click of button. Optionally, it can compress the merged file.

Fast and Easy to Use

With a very simple UI, pdfMerge does the required work in a very short span of time by saving the time to upload files online.

Secure

pdfMerge works locally on your device. This means that your files never leave your system, hence being safe and secure.
